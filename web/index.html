poker-trainer

Browse the solver's preflop charts GTO-Wizard-style — pick a table, walk the action line, read each hand off the 13×13 grid. Plus an equity calculator, a pot-odds drill, and the postflop GTO grid, all running in your browser.

Preflop charts

Browse the solved preflop trees (data/preflop/, computed by the in-repo preflop-gen MCCFR solver) GTO-Wizard-style: pick a table and stack depth, walk the action line, read each hand's mixed strategy off the 13×13 grid. Dimmed cells can't reach the spot; click a cell for frequencies and EV.

Equity calculator

Range-vs-range Monte-Carlo equity on a flop — the poker-trainer equity command. Defaults are the SRP BTN-vs-BB ranges the solution library uses.

Pot-odds drill

You're dealt a hand from a solved heads-up preflop equilibrium, villain bets — call or fold? Scored against your (Monte-Carlo) equity vs villain's whole range at this node, not one lucky hand, like drill pot-odds.

Flop equity explorer

Pick a heads-up preflop line — that fixes both players' arrival ranges — then enter your hand and any board (flop through river) to see your Monte-Carlo equity vs villain's whole range. Add a bet size for a pot-odds call/fold. No solve, so it's an equity read, not a betting strategy — that's the GTO grid below.

GTO strategy grid

Browse the committed starter-8 solutions as a 13×13 grid, like the table TUI. Cell colors are action frequencies averaged over the class's combos; click a cell for the per-combo breakdown.
